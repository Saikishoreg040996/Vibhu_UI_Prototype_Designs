My story

The reason I do this started with my mother

My journey into Medicare began while helping my mother through a serious illness. Like so many families, we discovered how overwhelming and confusing the system can be — the parts, the deadlines, the fine print.

The more I learned, the clearer it became that families needed someone to simplify the process and offer honest, unbiased guidance. That experience led me to become a licensed Medicare professional with one mission: to help individuals and families confidently navigate Medicare and choose coverage that fits their unique needs and budget.
