Our founder's voice

Carrying the message, near and far

As a cancer survivor herself, Dr. Gautami Tadimalla has carried the Foundation's message to national and global platforms — including the Union for International Cancer Control (UICC) and World Cancer Day — sharing her lived experience to inspire early detection, equitable care, and hope. Her advocacy continues to bring attention and dignity to the cause of cancer patients across India.
