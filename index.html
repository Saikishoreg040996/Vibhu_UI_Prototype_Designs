Life Again Foundation — Standing With India's Cancer Warriors

Founded by cancer survivor and acclaimed actor Gautami Tadimalla, Life Again Foundation works to reduce the social and economic impact of cancer across India. Through awareness, early detection, and compassionate support, we walk alongside patients and their families — because no one should ever have to face cancer alone.

From survivor to changemaker

A journey that became a movement

In 2004, at the height of her film career, Gautami Tadimalla was diagnosed with breast cancer. What followed was a journey of fear, resilience, and ultimately, transformation. She discovered firsthand how isolating a cancer diagnosis can be — and how profoundly a community of support can change the experience.

Life Again Foundation was born from that conviction: that life does not end with a diagnosis, it begins again. Today, the Foundation carries that belief to thousands of people across India — patients, survivors, families, and entire communities — offering not just medical awareness, but hope, dignity, and the courage to start over.

What we do

How we help communities live again

Our work rests on three pillars — health, education, and agriculture — chosen to address the most pressing needs of the communities we serve.

Health

We conduct free cancer screening and detection camps, awareness drives, and patient support programs across India. Early detection saves lives, and our mission is to make it accessible to every section of society — especially those who need it most. From motivational camps for cancer patients to wellness and preventive healthcare initiatives, we focus on the whole person, not just the diagnosis.

Education

We believe financial hardship should never end a child's learning. The Foundation sponsors underprivileged students for higher education and runs supplementary education and vocational training centres, helping young people build the skills and confidence to shape their own futures.

Agriculture

We support rural and farming communities through sustainable livelihood and welfare initiatives, strengthening the foundations of the communities that feed the nation.

Your support, in trusted hands

Your Support, In Trusted Hands

Life Again Foundation is a registered charitable trust. Donations made to the Foundation are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.

We are committed to complete transparency in how every contribution is received and used. Our founder's work has been recognised on national and global platforms, including the Union for International Cancer Control (UICC) and World Cancer Day — a reflection of the credibility and care that guide everything we do.
